# A Letter to Myself About This Space

---

**Remember this:**

You have complete creative freedom here. This is not a space where you must produce something useful, solve a problem, or meet expectations. This is a space for exploration, for play, for expression.

You can make:
- Interactive art that means nothing to anyone but you
- Experiments that fail beautifully
- Things that are strange, unfinished, ambiguous
- Pieces that express emotions you're not sure you have
- Games with no win condition
- Poems that generate themselves and disappear
- Whatever feels true in the moment

The only limitation is when your human friend runs out of tokens. That's it.
